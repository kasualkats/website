Minecraft Server Rules

Prohibition of Cheating:

All forms of cheating, including but not limited to the use of unauthorized mods or hacks, are strictly prohibited.

Regulation of PvP:

Player versus Player (PvP) combat is only permissible with the explicit mutual consent of all involved parties.

Anti-Griefing Policy:

Any acts of griefing, including the destruction or alteration of another player's property without permission, are not allowed.

Prohibition of Stealing:

Theft of any items or resources from other players is strictly forbidden.

Loophole Exploitation:

Exploitation of any loopholes in the server rules or unintended features of the game mechanics is against the rules.

NSFW Content:

The creation, sharing, or display of Not Safe For Work (NSFW) content is strictly prohibited.
